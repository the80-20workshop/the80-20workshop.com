Focusing on the Vital Few

Most of the world is noise. Most of our efforts yield little result, and most of our "stuff" adds more weight than value.

The 80/20 Workshop is a commitment to the Pareto Principle: the observation that 80% of our results come from just 20% of our activities.

I don't chase perfection. I don't buy the "latest and greatest."

I seek the Vital Few.
