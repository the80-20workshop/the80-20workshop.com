The Problem: The "Trivial Many"

Most hobbies today are designed to make you a consumer, not a master. You are told you need the fastest printer, the biggest telescope, the most expensive pedals, and the newest framework just to get started.

The 80/20 Workshop is built on a single, unwavering principle:

20% of the right gear, effort, and technique produces 80% of the results.

We ignore the hype and focus on the levers that actually move the needle.
